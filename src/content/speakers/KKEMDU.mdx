Daniel is an engineer at [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/company/what-we-do/) with experience developing Trading Systems, Risk Analytics, and applications for Financial Analysis of Equities and Fixed Income securities. He holds a Ph.D. in Molecular Biophysics from the University of Virginia, and was a CFA charter holder and member of the Chartered Financial Analyst Institute for more than 10 years. He is the Open Source maintainer of [Matplotlib's MPLFINANCE package](https://pypi.org/project/mplfinance/), and the author of McGraw-Hill's "Biophysics Demystified."
