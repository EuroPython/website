Martina serves as the Head of Data Engineering at Kiwi.com, where she oversees teams of analytics engineers and the data platform team. Her current focus lies in the adoption of the Data Mesh paradigm, driving the development of a self-service data platform for both batch and real-time data analytics, and setting up an ML platform to optimize data scientist workflows. Despite graduating with a degree in theoretical physics, Martina's career has consistently been linked to data engineering. Her experience spans creating data solutions for smart buildings to developing data warehouse solutions for major agencies.
