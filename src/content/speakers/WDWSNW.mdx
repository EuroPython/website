Andy Fundinger is a senior engineer at Bloomberg, where he develops Python
applications in the Data Gateway Platform team and supports Python developers
throughout the firm through the company's Python Guild. Andy has spoken several
times at PyGotham, as well as other conferences such as QCon, PyCaribbean, and
EuroPython.

In the past, Andy has worked on private equity and credit risk applications, web
services, and virtual worlds. Andy holds a master's degree in engineering from
Stevens Institute of Technology.
