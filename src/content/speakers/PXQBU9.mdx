Dr. Jodie Burchell is the Developer Advocate in Data Science at JetBrains, and
was previously a Lead Data Scientist at Verve Group Europe. After finishing a
PhD in Psychology and a postdoc in biostatistics, she has worked in a range of
data science and machine learning roles across natural language processing,
search improvement, recommendation systems, and programmatic advertising. She is
passionate about making Python data science and machine learning accessible for
others. She is also a long time content creator in data science, across
conference and user group presentations, books, webinars, and blogging.
