Jessica Temporal is a Sr Developer Advocate at Okta and co-founder of the [first Brazilian data science podcast, Pizza de Dados](https://pizzadedados.com/en). She’s also a LinkedIn Learning instructor. Creator of [GitFichas, a git study cards collection](https://gitfichas.com), and is a GitHub Star. Jess also authored ["The Big Git Microbook"](https://jtemporal.com/gitmicrobook). She is part of the Pyladies and in her free time, she knits, plays video games, and tries to learn to rollerblade.
